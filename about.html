About Precision

Where passion for perfection meets the art of automotive care.

Our Story

Precision Detailing was born from a simple obsession: the pursuit of automotive perfection. What started as a passion project — spending weekends perfecting paint finishes in a garage — evolved into Seattle's most trusted name in premium auto detailing.

We realized early on that most detailing services treat every vehicle the same. But a Tesla Model S demands different care than a Porsche 911. A daily driver needs a different approach than a weekend show car. That insight became our foundation.

Today, we serve the greater Seattle area's most discerning vehicle owners. From Bellevue to Tacoma, our clients trust us with their most prized possessions because they know we treat every vehicle with the same obsessive care we'd give our own.
